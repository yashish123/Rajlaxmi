About us

Rajlaxmi Developers group is one of the leading construction company in Vasai-Virar region.We are into this business since 10 years and have established our own identity in this industry. The group has scaled new heights by acquiring several properties and developing them independently and has carved niche for the quality of construction and modern amenities provided there.In each of the properties constructed have excellent elevation features and special care is taken to ensure that the customer gets the maximum coverage in terms of area available for usage. The company is associated with leading architects and designers who give shape to the dreams and ideas. The Group is in touch with the latest modern technology and is also associated with the leading companies for the various accessories provided in each of their projects. Personal attention is given to each of the items used in all their projects ensuring high quality of fittings and fixtures with minimum wear and tear. Finally the Group emphasizes in providing timely Possession of all its Properties completed in all respects and forming the Co-operative Societies as early as possible. Selling and finally handing over the possession of the flats marks just the beginning of the long association with our clients and we strongly believe in providing after sale services to each of the clients so that they are satisfied in all respects both as to the quality of workmanship and the accessories installed within the flats. The group has tied up with the leading financial institutions and provides active guidance and assistance to its clients. In short Rajlaxmi Group fulfills the aspirations of several people to help them acquire their DREAM HOUSES in the prime localities with all the modern day comforts and amenities provided therein. Our mission is providing Quality Construction,Timely Possession and Clear title for Future Price Appreciation. Our values comprises of Committed to honesty,Committed to trust and integrity and Committed to continuous improvement
